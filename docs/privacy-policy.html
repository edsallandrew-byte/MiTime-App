MiTime

Privacy Policy — Last updated April 19, 2026

MiTime does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All calculations happen entirely on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

MiTime does not collect, transmit, or store:

• Your name, email address, or any account information
• Your GPS coordinates or location history
• Your alarm settings or usage data
• Any analytics, crash reports, or telemetry
• Any information whatsoever

Location Access

MiTime requests access to your device’s location solely to determine your longitude. This longitude is used to calculate your personal solar time (MiTime) using a mathematical formula entirely on your device.

Your location is never sent to any server, never stored on your device beyond the current session, and never shared with any third party.

Notifications

MiTime requests notification permissions to fire alarms you set within the app. Alarm data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

MiTime does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children’s Privacy

MiTime is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date at the top.
